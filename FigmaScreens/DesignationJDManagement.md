# Designation & JD Management Screen

## Purpose
Manage designations and job descriptions.

## Fields
### Designation
- Title (required)
- Practice (dropdown, required)

### JD
- Title (required)
- Designation (dropdown, required)
- Description (textarea)
- Requirements (dynamic list)

## Actions
- Add/Edit/Delete Designation
- Add/Edit/Delete JD
