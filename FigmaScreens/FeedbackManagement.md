# Feedback Management Screen

## Purpose
Submit and review interview feedback.

## Fields
**Fields:**
- Candidate (auto)
- Panel Member (auto)
- Date (auto)
- Overall Rating (1-5 stars, required)
- Decision (dropdown: Cleared/Not Cleared/On Hold, required)
- Section Ratings (dynamic, from template)
- Comments (textarea, required)
- Observations (textarea, optional)
- Source (dropdown, required)

## Actions
- Submit Feedback
- Review Feedback
- View Details
